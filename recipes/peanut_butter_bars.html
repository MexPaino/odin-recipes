Peanut Butter Bars

Description

This recipe appears as "No-Bake Spicy Peanut Butter Squares" in Allrecipes Magazine and was modified to include Sriracha for an expected pop of spice.

Igridients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 Â½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter

Steps

  1. Mix together graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, butter or margarine, and 1 cup peanut butter in a medium bowl until well-blended.
    Press evenly into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13-inch pan.

  2. Place chocolate chips and 4 tablespoons peanut butter in a microwave-safe bowl.
    Microwave on high, stirring every 15 seconds, until smooth. Spread mixture over crust.

  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before cutting into 12 squares.
